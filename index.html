Hello, I’m Johannes and I’m a User Experience designer and problem solver from Gothenburg, Sweden. My passions is to transform visions and ideas into desirable, usable and smart digital products.

My passion for design, aesthetics, human behaviour and tehnology led me to recieve degrees in both product development and interaction design. The combination of these fields gave me a strong strategic design thinking which I daily utilize in my work to pinpoint and transform business missions, user values and objectives to digital solutions.

I also emphasize a user-centered approach in my work such as constructing user flows, wireframing, interactive prototypes and user testing. As a result my products are not only practical and efficient solutions but also desirable and enticing experiences!
